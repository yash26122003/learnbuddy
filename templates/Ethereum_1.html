What is ​ethereum?

  • Ethereum is one of the most popular decentralized, open-source blockchain with allows for the deployment of smart contracts
  • Users can interact on this network, with anyone having the freedom to deploy permanent and immutable DApps
  • Under Ethereum, one can create non-interchangeable tokens called NFTs (connections to digital works and real-world items) which can then be sold as unique digital properties
